E-Waste Safe Disposal

Understand the hazards of e-waste and learn how to dispose responsibly.

Battery

Extremely Hazardous

Why is it dangerous?

  • Lithium batteries can cause fires
  • Acid leaks contaminate soil & water
  • Contains heavy toxic metals
  • Releases harmful gases in landfills

Proper disposal

  • Recycle at battery centers
  • Use store-collection boxes
  • Tape damaged batteries
  • Do not throw in regular bins

CFL Bulb

Extremely Hazardous

Why is it dangerous?

  • Contains highly toxic mercury
  • Releases vapor when broken
  • Pollutes water sources
  • Affects lungs & nervous system

Proper disposal

  • Recycle at hardware stores
  • Wrap bulbs safely
  • Take to hazardous centers
  • Keep away from children

Power Bank

Extremely Hazardous

Why is it dangerous?

  • May explode under heat
  • High fire risk
  • Damaged units are dangerous
  • Chemicals pollute environment

Proper disposal

  • Recycle at e-waste centers
  • Drop-off at mobile stores
  • Handle damaged units carefully
  • Never burn or soak in water

Benefits of Proper Disposal

Environmental Protection

Prevents soil, water & air contamination.

Public Safety

Reduces poisoning, fire & explosion risks.

Resource Recovery

Valuable materials get reused & recycled.
