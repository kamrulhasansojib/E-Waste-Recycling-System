ABOUT US

Our E-Waste Recycling initiative started with a simple mission — to reduce electronic waste and promote eco-friendly recycling. We aim to create awareness about how discarded electronics can harm the environment if not handled properly.

We collect, recycle, and safely dispose of all types of e-waste, ensuring reusable parts are recovered and toxic materials are kept out of landfills. Together, we can make our planet cleaner and greener.
