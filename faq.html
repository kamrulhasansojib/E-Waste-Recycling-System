F A Q.?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about E-TRIEVE and e-waste recycling

What is E-TRIEVE and how does it work?

E-TRIEVE is an e-waste management platform that connects users with certified recycling companies. Simply submit your electronic waste details through our platform, select a pickup date, and our partner companies will collect your e-waste from your doorstep. We ensure your electronics are recycled responsibly and environmentally friendly.

What types of electronic devices can I recycle?

We accept a wide range of electronic devices including computers, laptops, mobile phones, tablets, televisions, monitors, printers, keyboards, mice, cables, chargers, batteries, and other electronic accessories. Both working and non-working devices are accepted.

Is the pickup service free?

Yes! Our e-waste pickup service is completely free of charge. We believe in making e-waste recycling accessible to everyone. There are no hidden fees or charges for collection, transportation, or recycling of your electronic waste.

How do you ensure my data is secure?

We recommend that you backup and delete all personal data from your devices before pickup. Our partner companies follow strict data destruction protocols and can provide certified data wiping services upon request. All hard drives and storage devices are securely destroyed to prevent any data recovery.

How quickly can I schedule a pickup?

You can schedule a pickup as early as the next business day. Simply fill out the submission form, select your preferred date, and our partner company will confirm your appointment. In most cases, pickups are completed within 2-3 business days.

Do I get paid for my old electronics?

Depending on the condition and type of your electronics, you may receive compensation or earn points through our rewards system. Working devices in good condition typically have higher value. After assessment, our partner companies will inform you if your items qualify for payment.

Why is e-waste recycling important?

E-waste contains harmful materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium that can pollute soil and water if not disposed properly. Recycling recovers valuable materials like gold, silver, and copper, reducing the need for mining. Proper e-waste management protects our environment and conserves natural resources for future generations.

Which areas do you serve?

We currently serve major cities and their surrounding areas. You can check the "Nearby Companies" section in your dashboard to see which recycling companies operate in your location. We're continuously expanding our network to reach more areas.

Are your recycling companies certified?

Yes! All our partner companies are certified and follow environmental regulations for e-waste recycling. They comply with local and international standards to ensure safe handling, processing, and disposal of electronic waste while minimizing environmental impact.

What happens after I submit a request?

After submitting your request, the selected company will review it and confirm your pickup appointment. You'll receive a notification with pickup details. On the scheduled date, their team will arrive at your address to collect the items. You can track the status of your request in your dashboard at any time.
